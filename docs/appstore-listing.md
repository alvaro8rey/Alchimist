## Descripción completa
```
Start with just four elements — Fire, Water, Earth, and Air — and combine them to discover over 3,000 unique creations.

Drag elements together on an infinite canvas and watch what happens. Some combinations are obvious. Others will surprise you. And some? No one has ever found them before.

── FEATURES ──

∙ 3,000+ elements to discover through experimentation
∙ AI-powered combinations — if a recipe doesn't exist yet, the game creates it on the spot
∙ Be the FIRST in the world to discover a new element and claim it as yours
∙ Global leaderboard — compete to discover more elements than anyone else
∙ Global Feed — see first discoveries from players around the world in real time
∙ Infinite zoomable canvas — place and combine as many elements as you want
∙ Combination history — review your last experiments
∙ Works offline with hundreds of built-in recipes

── HOW TO PLAY ──

1. Tap an element from your inventory to place it on the canvas
2. Drag one element onto another to combine them
3. Discover the result — and keep going

There's no wrong answer. Every combination is worth trying.

── COMPETE ──

Create an account to save your progress, appear on the global leaderboard, and claim first discoveries. Or play as a guest — no sign-up required.

Start combining. The periodic table of imagination awaits.
```
